Pathways

Computer Science/Engineering

The DeWitt Clinton computer science program features a 4-year course where students will become proficient in building websites through partnerships with companies like Concorde Education and Syracuse University.

MACY Honors

The DeWitt Clinton MACY Honors program is a partnership with MACY college to encourage early graduation in students by awarding them college credits and helping students take on intense classes that prepare students for the rigor of college.

Health/Nursing

The DeWitt Clinton health/nursing program helps students explore the medical field by introducing them to and honing their knowledge in iatric subjects, while also getting them top-notch education via partnerships with hospitals like Montefiore.

Dance

The DeWitt Clinton dance program teaches students the art of dance through rigorous coursework and encouragement for creativity, enabling the self-expression of students while also instilling them with the skills needed to pursue a dance career in the future.
